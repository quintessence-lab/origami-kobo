## What You Need

- 1 sheet of duo origami paper (red/green, 15cm × 15cm)
- A flat surface
- Marker (optional, for drawing seeds)

## Instructions

1. Place the green side up and fold into a triangle
2. Fold the left and right corners up to the top point
3. Fold each flap back down slightly (these become the leaves)
4. Flip over (the red side is now showing)
5. Fold the left and right corners to the center
6. Fold the bottom corner up slightly
7. Tuck the side corners behind to round out the shape

## Finishing Touch

Use a white pen or correction pen to draw small dots for the seeds.

## Tips

- **Duo paper**: Red/green duo paper is ideal, but you can layer two sheets if needed
- **Size**: Using small paper (7.5cm) creates a cute accessory-sized strawberry
- **Arrangement**: Make several and string them into a garland

## Fun Fact

The small bumps on a strawberry's surface aren't actually seeds — each one is a tiny individual fruit! The red part we eat is actually the swollen receptacle of the flower.
