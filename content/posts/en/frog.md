## What You Need

- 1 sheet of origami paper (15cm × 15cm)
- A flat surface

## Instructions

1. Fold the paper in half vertically, then unfold
2. Fold the top corners to create triangles, then collapse the top into a triangle shape
3. Fold the left and right corners of the triangle up to the top point
4. Fold those flaps outward to create the front legs
5. Fold the bottom half up toward the top
6. Fold it back down to create an accordion fold (this forms the back legs and spring)
7. Flip over and you're done!

## Tips

- **The accordion fold**: A crisp accordion fold is the key to a high-jumping frog
- **Paper thickness**: Slightly thicker paper makes the frog jump better
- **How to play**: Press down on the back half with your finger and release quickly

## Fun Fact

This origami frog is also known as the "jumping frog" and is enjoyed worldwide. You can even have competitions to see whose frog jumps the farthest!
