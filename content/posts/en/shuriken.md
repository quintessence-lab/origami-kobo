## What You Need

- 2 sheets of origami paper (different colors recommended, 15cm × 15cm)
- A flat surface

## Instructions (Part A)

1. Fold the paper in half vertically
2. Fold in half horizontally, crease, and unfold
3. Fold the top half to the right and the bottom half to the left (Z-shape)
4. Fold the top section into a triangle pointing down, and the bottom into a triangle pointing up

## Instructions (Part B)

1. Follow the same steps as Part A, but reverse the direction in step 3
2. Fold the top half to the left, bottom half to the right
3. Reverse the triangle fold directions as well

## Assembly

1. Flip Part B over
2. Place Part A on top of Part B in a cross shape
3. Tuck Part B's triangles into Part A's pockets
4. Flip over and tuck the remaining triangles in
5. Press firmly and you're done!

## Tips

- **Color combinations**: High-contrast pairs like black/silver or red/blue look great
- **Fold direction**: Getting A and B reversed will prevent assembly, so pay attention
- **Tight tucks**: Push the triangles in firmly so the star holds together

## Fun Fact

While shuriken are famous as ninja weapons, they were actually used as a supplementary self-defense tool rather than a primary weapon. The origami shuriken has been a beloved classic among Japanese children for generations.
