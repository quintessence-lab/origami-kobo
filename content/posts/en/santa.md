## What You Need

- 1 sheet of origami paper (red, 15cm × 15cm)
- Marker (for drawing the face)
- A flat surface

## Instructions

1. Place white side up and fold into a triangle

![Step 1](/images/origami/steps/santa/step-1.svg)

2. Unfold, fold along the other diagonal, and unfold again

![Step 2](/images/origami/steps/santa/step-2.svg)

3. Fold one corner to the center (this becomes the hat brim)

![Step 3](/images/origami/steps/santa/step-3.svg)

4. Fold that section back slightly (creates the white trim)

![Step 4](/images/origami/steps/santa/step-4.svg)

5. Flip over

![Step 5](/images/origami/steps/santa/step-5.svg)

6. Fold the left and right corners to meet at the center

![Step 6](/images/origami/steps/santa/step-6.svg)

7. Fold the bottom corner up (this becomes Santa's beard)

![Step 7](/images/origami/steps/santa/step-7.svg)

8. Fold the tip back down slightly

![Step 8](/images/origami/steps/santa/step-8.svg)

9. Tuck the side edges behind to shape the figure

![Step 9](/images/origami/steps/santa/step-9.svg)


## Finishing Touch

Draw eyes and a nose with a marker. A round red nose makes it extra cute!

## Tips

- **Paper color**: Red paper with a white reverse side works perfectly
- **Face proportions**: Small eyes and a large round nose look the cutest
- **Display ideas**: Attach a string to hang on a Christmas tree, or glue onto cards

## Fun Fact

Santa Claus is based on Saint Nicholas, a 4th-century Christian bishop. The modern image of Santa in a red suit became widespread through Coca-Cola's 1931 advertising campaign.
